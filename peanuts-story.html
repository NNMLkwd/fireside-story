It was a dark and stormy night...

Woodstock was snoring so loud, that he was drowning out the storm, and poor Snoopy was unable to go back to sleep.

Snoopy wished for a bowl of warm milk but didn't have a hot plate.

Meanwhile, indoors, Charlie Brown was staring into the storm. In his mind the rolling thunder clouds and pouring rain were not a storm but his inner ennui.



Sally, two blocks away, was also unable to sleep, not due to angst but excitement from another football stunt.

Charlie had fallen for it again.
